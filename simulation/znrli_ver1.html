INSTRUCTION

  1. Connect the components as mentioned below:
    L1-L3, L4-L7, L8-L15, L15-L10, L11-L17, L16-L18, L17-L5, L18-L6, L13-L15, L14-L19, L9-L16, L12-L9.(For eg. click on 1 and then drag to 3 and so on.)
  2. Click on 'Check Connection' button to check the connections.
  3. If connected wrong, double click on the wrong connection. Else click on 'Delete all connection' button to erase all the connections.
  4. Set the Zener Voltage(VZ)= 5.1 v
  5. Set the Series Resistance (RS) = 1 kΩ
  6. Set the Load Resistance (RL) = 2 kΩ
  7. Vary DC voltage from 0 to 20 v.
  8. Voltmeter is placed parallel to load resistor and ammeter series with the series resistor.
  9. Choose appropriate DC voltage such that zener diode is 'on'.
  10. Now note the Voltmeter and Ammeter reading for various DC voltage.
  11. Note the Load current(IL), zener current(IZ), Output voltage(VO)
  12. Calculate the voltage regulation.
  13. Repeat the experiment for Load Resistance (RL) = 5 kΩ
